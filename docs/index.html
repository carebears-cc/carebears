How CareBears Helps

We provide the tools, resources, and support patients and caregivers need to navigate healthcare with confidence and compassion.

📋

Medical Record Summaries

We translate complex medical terminology into plain language, making it easier to understand diagnoses, treatment plans, and next steps.

🗓️

Appointment Management

Keep track of all appointments across multiple providers in one place, with helpful reminders and preparation instructions.

🔍

Resource Navigator

Find local support groups, patient assistance programs, and educational resources tailored to specific conditions.

👨‍⚕️

Second Opinion Facilitator

Get guidance on obtaining second opinions with prepared medical history summaries and specialist recommendations.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Care Community Builder

Connect with other caregivers and patients facing similar situations to share knowledge and support.

❤️

Emotional Support

Access 24/7 supportive guidance during difficult moments when you need a virtual bear hug the most.
